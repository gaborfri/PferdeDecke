Placeholder icons.

To use the PferdeLog logo as the PWA icon, add two PNG files here:
- icon-192.png (192x192)
- icon-512.png (512x512)

Both are referenced by manifest.json and index.html. After adding, reload the app,
then remove and re-add the Home Screen icon on iOS to see the change.
